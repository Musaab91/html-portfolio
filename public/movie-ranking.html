My top 3 series of all-time

Black Mirror

My favourite Sci-fi series. Shows the dystopian reality of the consequences of technology.

Peaky Blinders

A family from Birmingham make their way through trying to legalize their mafia businesses.

Silicon Valley

A group of programmers trying to start their own company but are always faced with consequences which reflects the reality of how difficult it is to start a business, in an entertaining way.
